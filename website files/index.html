Introducing: Piano Gloves™

The one-of-a-kind experience allowing you to play piano on any surface

Put on the gloves, press calibrate to set where the piano will be, push your fingers against that space and hear as the gloves play accurately the correct key.

Piano Gloves™ come in three standard sizes to give everyone the best experience, sizes include small, medium, and large. Custom sizes are available upon request.

Realfeel™ is a brand new state-of-the-art technology equipped in the Piano Gloves™ to make it feel as if you are actually pushing a real piano key.

Play with confidence knowing that Piano Gloves™ have a 4 hour battery life, with recharging to 100% taking as little as 2 hours and 1 hour of use takes only 7 minutes.
